PRAYER FOR DIVINE VICTORY IN THE FINAL BATTLE OF ARMAGEDON


▶ Page copied from website ▶️ www.eusouluz.com.br ◀️


The prayer below is very powerful and complete. We are living the final battle between good and evil on Earth! Whoever prays this prayer every day will be part of the joint effort of the Ascended Masters of the Great White Fraternity, to save the planet and the children of Light!


Participate with the masters! Get to work! Your word is the strength that masters need to overcome the forces of evil, which are so frightening and harming the children of light on this planet!


The Victory Always of the Light!

Paulo R Simões

In the name of my Mighty I AM Presence and beloved Divine Mother and by the Power of the Threefold Flame in my heart,/ I decree, offer and accept the holy and eternal prayer for Divine Victory in the battle of Armageddon:


FATHER, I AM YOUR KINGDOM MANIFESTED,
I AM ON EARTH AS I AM IN HEAVEN!


I accept and affirm God's coming to Earth as a Reality here and now. I declare that all human imperfections / manifested in disagreement with the perfect divine plan created for planet Earth / must now disappear, by the all-correcting Power of the Holy Spirit's Light.


I beg and command the immediate disclosure of all that is done secretly, dishonest, incorrect/ and at variance with the highest claim and purpose of our Father's divine plan.


I demand and demand correct action from the children of Light,/ acting with justice to expose the errors of the evil ones,/ exposing all their conspiracies and strategies to enslave and subject humans.


I invoke the Angels of Mighty Victory,/ the Elohim Hercules and Amazonia, Astrea and Pureza, Arturos and Vitória,/ the Archangel Michael and his legions of angels of the first ray,/ to free humanity from these oppressors,/ incarnate or no,/ and I declare with our Lord Jesus Christ/ that


"The earth belongs to the Lord God in all its fullness"


"Brazil belongs to the Lord God in all its fullness"


"Brasília belongs to the Lord God in all its fullness"


"The Brazilian Government belongs to the Lord God in all its fullness"


Your Kingdom come, Lord! (3x)


That Your Kingdom may come speedily,/ I affirm that Your Will is being fulfilled, on Earth as it is in Heaven.


I AM, I AM and I AM / the Whirlwind Action of the Holy Spirit, dissolving and consuming all the poison of human consciousness / and transmuting by the Infinite and Victorious Power of the Light of God / all substances of human creation, which have resulted in a flood of unscrupulous products to the minds of the world's youth.


I declare that everything and everyone who seeks to imprison or enslave the minds of humans─/ whether in politics, theater, literature, art, the Internet, radio, television, cinema/ or labeled as such ─/ and all evil imperfections and stereotypes of human creation/ be placed in the Spiritual Flame, to be consumed by the Fires of Heaven,/ assisting the spiritual intelligences,/ who, together with our beloved Ascended Master Jesus Christ,/ seek to bring the Kingdom of God for mankind.


I periodically offer this prayer for Divine Victory in the battle of Armageddon. Therefore, I decree in the name of the Cosmic Christ, the beloved Lord Maitreya:


The Kingdom of God that seeks to manifest Love everywhere in the world, the perfection of the Good, the Good, the Beautiful, the Love, the Order and the Progress / should be, will be and is established, here and now , among humans.


Therefore/ the fruits of this realization of the Cosmic Christ/ must manifest, according to the spoken word, which I declare here and now, for the sake of my life and the life of all the children of light.

p>

So I say: Beloved God, Father-Mother,/ Alpha and Omega,/ remove the veil of ignorance that has blinded humanity, preventing them from seeing the right course of action and keeping them trapped in desires that they enslave the soul.


May the chains forged by human ignorance/ be finally broken and consumed by the Holy Fire of Your Holy Spirit,/ O God.


- Exercise your dominion now, Lord,/ over the whole Earth and its evolutions,/ over all my thoughts and feelings,/ ambitions and desires,/ aspirations and plans.


- Exercise Your dominion now, Lord,/ over the Federal Supreme Court,/ the Superior Court of Justice,/ the Superior Electoral Court,/ the Federal Regional Courts;/ and over all judges and judges/, Public Prosecutors Justice, Public Prosecutors and the Brazilian Bar Association.


- Exercise your dominion now, Lord,/ over the Federal Government, the National Congress, and all public service bodies to the population,/ over all political parties, unions and NGOs in Brazil,/ over the Navy, the Brazilian Army and Air Force,/ and on the Police: Federal, Civil and Military throughout Brazil.


- Exercise your dominion now, Lord,/ over schools and colleges,/ over city halls and governments of all states,/ over all hospitals and health clinics.


Lord God,/ I wish and decree that all those who misuse power,/ in justice; in the federal, state and municipal governments;/ in the armed forces and in the police;/ in health and education;/ be plucked by the hand of God, from their positions of power, and brought to the judgment of the Lords of Karma now, Lord!< /p>

For their work positions I ask, the replacement by responsible children of light and of goodwill towards the Brazilian people!


- Exercise Your dominion now, Lord,/ over the leaders of countries that seek war;/ over the wars and threats of war in the Middle East and across the planet;/ over terrorists around the world;/ about abortionists and abortionists of unborn children;/ about Brazilian prisons, organized crime, drug traffickers and also about users./ Take them to the judgment of the Lords of Karma now, Lord!

- Exercise Your dominion now, Lord,/ over those who falsely present themselves as representatives of the Great White Fraternity,/ trying to distort and defame the teachings of the Ascended Masters. Take them to the Lords of Karma judgment now, Lord!


- Exercise Your dominion now, Lord,/ over my workplace and over all the activities of the ascended masters, led by sincere chelas,/ in Brazil and all over the planet.


- Exercise Your dominion now, Lord,/ over the climate of Brazil and the whole Earth, harmonizing the elementals of time, so that the children of light of goodwill are spared from the planetary karmic return.


- Exercise Your dominion now, Lord, over ..... (your personal requests).


Dear Lord God,/ many are united with me, praying this same prayer, on this very day!/ So, Desire and Decree it with us Lord,/ and it will become a reality!


Make the crystalline and transparent image of Your perfection/ form around me,/ by the Sacred Fire,/ by the divine Power of God,/ by the image of the Cosmic Christ/ and by universal love.


So that I AM, here, now and forever, a perfect Focus,/ for and on behalf of the spiritual hierarchy of this planet;/ for and on behalf of the Great White Fraternity;/ for and on behalf of the Beloved Ascended Masters: Jesus Christ, Kuthumi, Saint Germain, El Morya, the Beloved Virgin Mary/ and all those who serve with them in the Great White Fraternity, for the liberation of the Earth and its evolutions.


I AM, I AM and I AM,/ by all the Love of God I know that I AM/ the utter perfection of the image of Divine Eden,/ the enveloping radiance of the Golden Age of Illumination, which instantly removes all the barriers to the Kingdom of God that now manifests / and forever establishes the Mighty Perfection of Divine Genius in the center of my heart's Altar Flame.


I AM here, now and forever / a part of God, a manifestation of God, a child of Light!


I AM affirming this same goal and objective for all children of light on this planet/ and stand as an advocate for the faithful. So help me, O God, Our Father! Help me, O Beloved Jesus, Our Lord! May the Powers of Light and Infinite Grace help me!


Therefore/ establish, O God, Your Magnificent Perfection over all the Earth, so that Your Perpetual Kingdom may manifest, shining in full radiance over this Beloved planet!


Now and forever,
Thy Kingdom come, Lord!
Thy Kingdom come, Lord!
Thy Kingdom come, Lord!


For I wish that everything happens according to Your will!
So be it, in the name of the Father, the Mother, the Son and the Holy Spirit!


Amen!
