I suggest to those who are having unpleasant experiences to remove consciously all the power they gave to these conditions, without knowing it. When it is necessary to discuss some condition to understand it, withdraw, from immediately, whatever power was given to her afterwards, know that: "I AM THE HARMONIOUS PRESENCE THAT ALWAYS PREVAILS ABOVE ANY CONDITION" .
I will repeat, again, something I hinted at earlier, but which was only partially understood: "Anyone, especially the student who experienced disharmony or limitations in your mind, home, or world, you may with a persistent effort and without strain, firmly stand firm in the following decree to keep your home free from anything undesirable: "I AM, the ruling Presence directing in perfect Divine Order, commanding the Harmony, Happiness and Presence of God's opulence CREATOR in my life, my mind, my home and my world” .
When I say "I AM the Governing Presence" I am completely sure aware that I have set in motion all the Power and Intelligence of God the CREATOR to produce the desired conditions and that these will be self-sustained.
It seems to Me that you have not clearly understood that when you use the expression: "I AM the Presence in my mind, home and world", not only you are commanding the Conquering Presence of this activity through your your own conscience, but you are also invoking the assistance of the Presence of God the "I AM" in the home and in the world of those who contact him with Her. This is vital for the student to understand. Don't be discouraged if you don't feel the immediate manifestation of this harmony that you desire. I kept feeling the Conquering Presence "I AM".
Do you not see that when you are in this Consciousness, only the Presence of which are you aware of? Any other external activity that is not desirable, it is only a distorted activity of this Magna Energy. Therefore, when you say: "I AM the Conquering Presence, I command this I AM Presence that perfectly governs my mind, home, affairs, and world" , you will have issued the greatest Decree that can be made, and you have only than to feel the sustaining power of it when you face any appearance. Thus you will find Perfection manifested in your mind, home and world.
I would want students to read this special part every day, so that would keep before them the Mighty Truth which backs up these statements.
